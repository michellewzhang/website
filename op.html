overview

The intersection of art and computing fascinates me. Since 2018, I have independently created multiple sketches on Open Processing, which is an online platform and community that allows for the creation of creative, visual programs.

My sketches involve a combination of computer-generated art, randomization (including random word and color generators), algorithmic design, and user interaction with keyboard and mouse.

Open Processing uses the p5.js library of JavaScript, which is a self-taught skill I continue to develop in my spare time.
